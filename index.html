About MCF

Migrant Children's Foundation (MCF) is a non-profit organization based in Beijing, dedicated to supporting the education, health, and well-being of children in need. We work directly with local schools, volunteers, and donors to create meaningful opportunities and lasting impact.

Every item you purchase directly contributes to our projects including comprehensive health checks, educational enrichment programs, and seasonal events for children in need. Your support helps us create brighter futures.
